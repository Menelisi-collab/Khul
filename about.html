Khulead is an educational directory and resource. In many cases,finding the right field of study can be a daunting task for an aspiring student. Even more daunting, is the aspect of funding the studies. Khulead seeks to aliviate these challanges by providing financial aid and relevant information on pursued fields of study. Serving as a necessary companion, Khulead becomes a bridge that connects the prospect(s) to tertiary institutions in a clearer way.

A Student enrolls as a member to Khulead and pays a monthly subscrition between R500-R800. Functioning in a manner similar to 'medical aid' and funeral cover, Khulead then ensures it's members are carefully advised on possible educational paths to pursue, while guaranteeing to cover tuition according to the funds available for said member(s).
